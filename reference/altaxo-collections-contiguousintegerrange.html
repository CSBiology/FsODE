ContiguousIntegerRange Type

Namespace: Altaxo.Collections
Assembly: AltaxoCore.Redist.dll
Base Type: ValueType
All Interfaces: IContiguousIntegerRange , IAscendingIntegerCollection , IReadOnlyList<int> , IReadOnlyCollection<int> , IEnumerable<int> , IEnumerable , ICloneable
Kind: Struct

Represents a range of consecutive integers. The range can be empty (in this case both ContiguousIntegerRange.Start as well as ContiguousIntegerRange.Count are zero).
